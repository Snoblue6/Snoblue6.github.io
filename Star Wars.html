Star Wars

New Republic (Post-Hossin Prime)

(Non-Mainstream Cannon)

Hossnin Prime may have been destroyed but a new government (surviving senators and newly elected or chosen senators) has formed on Corellia.

Resistance

(Star Wars)

The Resistance is the main opposition the the First Order and then the Final Order back by the New Republic (covertly).

New Republic (Pre-Hossin Prime)

(Star Wars)

Despite gaining dominance over most of the galaxy, threats still loom. Unfortunately for the New Republic leadership is complacent and doubt or ignore the threat in the Unknown Reasons that is steadily growing larger. 

Rebel Alliance

(Star Wars)

The Alliance to Restore the Republic goal is to overthrow the Empire and restore the Galactic Republic which was ended in a self-coup by its last chancellor.

Delegation of 2,000

(Star Wars)

This delegation was a group of senators in the Republic Senate. They wanted the Chancellor toward the end of the Clone Wars to give up his emergency powers given after the war is over. 

First Order (Snoke)

(Star Wars)

This remnant of the Empire went into the Unknown Regions becoming a powerful faction. They would seize alot of power after Star Killer Base after striking a significant blow to the New Republic and Resistance.

First Order (Kylo Ren)

(Star Wars)

Kylo Ren eventually launched a successful coup against Supreme Leader Snoke becoming Supreme Leader himself. However not all (Pro-Old Guard General Hux) were in support of Kylo Ren and at least one (Hux) actively work against Kylo Ren (though this did not mean they were sympathizers to the Resistance (just wanted Kylo Ren to lose).

Final Order

(Star Wars)

The Final Order (lead by SURPRISE! Palpatine) was a combination of the initial fleet, First Order, Knights of Ren, and other dark side warriors. It was brought down at the Battle of Exegol.

Imperial Remnant

(Non-Mainstream Cannon)

Though the New Republic became dominant in the Galaxy this did not mean that that the Empire simply disappear nor does it mean that all Imperials join the First Order.  Byss and some other worlds were still part of the Imperial Remnant. Lead by some Imperial Leader (not an Emperor: more on the lines of Pellaon in the Legends). The Imperial Remnant would become more moderate on training than the Empire (promoting ideas of patriotism and not nearly brain washing their soldiers). However they clash with the New Republic, Resistance, and First Order on occasion. They have allies in an area called the New Mexican Sector.

Galactic Empire

(Star Wars)

The Galactic Empire was formed when Chancellor Palpatine declared himself Emperor with the approval "though not unanimous" of the Senate. The Imperial Senate was a rubber stamping body (though Senators like the Organas remain in opposition), while the Emperor held all the power. When the Senate was dissolved it fell to the Regional Governors to have power under Palpatine.

Galactic Republic

(Star Wars)

The Galactic Republic by the time of the Clone Wars had suffer many problems. A major one was corruption. Some planets eventually would try to to secede from the Republic as the CIS. This would lead to the current chancellor (Palpatine) taking emergency power (which he wouldn't relinquish when the war was done) given to him by the Senate. The Senate however unlike the Imperial Senate was more independent and less of a rubber stamping committee whose members however were not always elected to their post (Naboo, Onderon, Alderaan, and Mon Calmari). Some planets even opted out of the war (Corellia) with it not taking an active part of the Senate until the Clone Wars was over.

Confederacy of Independent Systems

(Star Wars)

The CIS were worlds that seceded from the Republic (such as Umbara and Raxus) some worlds (like Rodia and Onderon) temporarily seceded from the Republic. They were lead by Count Dooku who lead the Separatist Parliament. They also include non-Republic worlds (like Geonosis and Zygeria) as allies.
